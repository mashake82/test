Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

How can we expect to meet the chauffeur at the airport?

If you have provided a mobile number, the chauffeur will send a text message just after the flight lands. If you have requested the meet and greet service, the chauffeur will hold a sign or a name board. If for some reason you can't find us, please call +44(0)2086604040 /+44(0)7402993983 or the chauffeur driver's mobile number provided with the booking confirmation. We kindly request that you don't leave the terminal before finding us.

What happens if my flight details change?

Please inform us by email or phone with your new details, and we will update your booking accordingly.

What happens if my flight details change, and I do not have new flight details?

Please inform us by email or phone and keep in touch until you have the new flight details.

What happens if my flight is delayed?

We will be monitoring your flight and the chauffeur. The chauffeur will be at the pickup location 15 minutes earlier than the flight landing time to save you from waiting.

If my flight arrives early, will my chauffeur be there?

Yes, we always monitor your flight to ensure your chauffeur and vehicle arrive on time, even if your flight is early.

Are parking and toll charges included in the fare?

No, parking and toll charges are not included and will be added to your final invoice.

What is "Meet and Greet"?

"Meet and Greet" is a service where the chauffeur will be at the airport, holding a board with your name or your company name. They will greet you outside the Arrivals area, assist you with your luggage, and show you to your car.

Will my chauffeur respect my privacy and confidentiality?

Absolutely, all of our drivers have signed a non-disclosure agreement to guarantee your privacy and confidentiality.
